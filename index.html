Staffordshire Stormers Touch

Our mission is to grow elite touch rugby participation in Staffordshire and the midlands.

Each player has an appetite and commitment to becoming an elite player, and to promote a progression pathway for all players.

Training

Training is every Wednesday from 1900 until 2030 kindly hosted for free by Stafford Rugby Club. Occasionally alternative training locations will be used to suit players from different clubs.

Everyone gets their first session free! After that Stafford Touch Club members can train for free, everyone else is £3 per session.

Each session will comprise of a team warm-up, skills, drills, a game format and a team cool down.

FAQs

How much does membership / training cost?

Whilst training at Stafford Rugby Club, training is free for Stafford RFC members. Other can pay £3 on the night. There are no membership costs. Each training session costs £4 and is payable on the night.

I already play for another team, can I also play / train with you?

Yes, every member of our team has a local "home" club.

Stormers is not in competion with local clubs playing in local leagues. Our team has been created to aid players that want to take the next step, to play in the national NTS competitions, or aim for regional or national squad selection. We welcome everyone that wants to improve their skills and spread their knowledge to their home clubs.

We have an ambition to compete in the NTS each each, and gain promotion to play in divisions 1/2.

What ages can train and play?

All players aged 14+ are welcome.

Occasionally younger players are invited that have good playing experience (for example regional Wildcats or national HP squads) and a need to train at a higher level than offered at their local club.

If you are younger than 14 but would like to be considered please contact us

Do I need to be a registered ETA member?

You don't need to be a registered ETA player to train, but you will need to be registered to play in the NTS tournaments.
