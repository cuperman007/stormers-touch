Staffordshire Stormers Touch Rugby

Our mission is to grow elite touch rugby participation in Staffordshire and the midlands.

Each player has an appetite and commitment to becoming an elite player, and to promote a progression pathway for all players.

Training

Training is usually on a Thursday night from 1900 until 2030. Locations will vary to suit players from different clubs.

For the latest schedule head over to Spond for locations, dates, times and payment.

Everyone gets their first session free

We take training seriously and each session will comprise of a team warm-up, skills, drills, game format and a team cool down.

FAQs

How much does membership / training cost?

There are no membership costs. Each training session costs Â£4 and is payable on Spond

I already play for another team, can I also play / train with you?

Yes

Every member of our team has a local "home" club. Stormers is not in competion with local clubs playing in local leagues. We welcome everyone that wants to improve their skills and spread their knowledge to their home clubs. We have an ambition to compete in the DNTS in 2023 and the NTS in the following years.

What ages can train and play?

All players aged 14+ are welcome.

Occasionally younger players are invited that have good playing experience (for example regional Wildcats or national HP squads) and a need to train at a higher level than offered at their local club.

If you are younger than 14 but would like to be considered please contact us

Do I need to be a registered ETA member?

You don't need to be a registered ETA player to train, but you will need to be registered to play in any national tournaments.
